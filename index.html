Meet Mike Annuzzi

Mike Annuzzi (Pop/Blues) is a Silicon Valley based singer & songwriter. He performed at 200+ shows in 2014 and is a resident performer in the SF Bay Area, California Central Coast, and Napa Valley.

Mike has a positive & rhythmic style and has performed alongside names like The Cure, Outkast, Eric Church, Los Lonely Boys, Kenny Wayne Shepherd, Colbie Caillat, Jackie Greene, Tyrone Wells, Edwin McCain, and more.

He is currently working on his 3rd album release.
